# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## UPI & Cybercrime Safety Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Always verify payment requests before approving  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Use only trusted and official payment apps  
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Check receiver details carefully before sending money  
- Do not trust messages offering rewards or prizes  
- Regularly monitor your bank transactions  
- Report cyber fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the helpline number 1930 in case of emergency
