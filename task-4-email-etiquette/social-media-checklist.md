# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Keep your profile professional  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Respect others while posting or commenting  
- Use privacy settings to protect your data  

## Don’ts
- Don’t share OTP, passwords, or personal details  
- Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content  
- Don’t click on suspicious links  
- Don’t spread fake or unverified information  
- Don’t overshare private information
